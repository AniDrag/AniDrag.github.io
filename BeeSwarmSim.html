Project discription

This was my 3. group project at university, where we created a multiplayer bee swarm simulator game in Unity. The game focuses on collecting pollin, quest completion, and upgrading your bee swarm as you progress through the game world. Players can collect resources, complete various quests, and engage in mini-games to enhance their gameplay experience. My primary role in this project was to develop core gameplay systems, Bee statemachine, new bee creation tooling, Shop system, Bee types and skill framework. This project allowed me to apply my skills in C# programming and Unity development while collaborating effectively with my team members.
