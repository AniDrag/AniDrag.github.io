Project discription

This was my 2. group project at university, where we created a vampire survival type game in Unity. The game focuses on shooting enemies, surviving waves, highscore, and managing upgrades, Its a rouglike with not a lot of functionality but has its core gameplay. Developed as a team of 6 over a period of 3 weeks. My contributions to the project included developing core gameplay systems such as the boss fight mechanics, player controls, wawenemy spawning system, and various gameplay features. Tooling for level design and enemy placement, and designer friendly systems.
